About Us

The Pakistan Air Force (PAF) (Urdu: پاک فِضائیہ, romanized: Pāk Fìzāʾiyah; pronounced [pɑːk fɪzɑːɪjəɦ]) is the aerial warfare branch of the Pakistan Armed Forces, tasked primarily with the aerial defence of Pakistan, with a secondary role of providing air support to the Pakistan Army and Navy.The Pakistan Air Force is the air warfare branch of the Pakistan Armed Forces who are tasked with providing, in synergy with other services, the most efficient, assured and cost-effective aerial Defence of Pakistan.
